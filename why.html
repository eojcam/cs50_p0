How I Got Here:

Several years ago, after returning from a position as Orchestral Guitarist on Royal Caribbean Cruise Lines ship the "Monarch of the Seas," I decided to take some Classical Guitar lessons with a private teacher. I then began to learn how to play the guitar without a pick...revealing some new possibilities, like playing Walking Bass Lines, Chords, and Melody, all at once!

Finger Style Pick
Thumb and Fingers work independently. Only one attack possible.
Invented first, 😎 (technique has been developed for a much longer time.) Reliance, such that if you drop it, (or don't have one), means you are "out of business!"
